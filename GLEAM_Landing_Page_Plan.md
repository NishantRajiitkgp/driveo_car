## Section 18 — FAQ

**Section label:** `FREQUENTLY ASKED`

**Headline:**

> Got questions? We've got answers.

**Accordion items (10 questions):**

**How does GLEAM work?**
> You book a service through our website. A certified GLEAM provider is assigned to your job, drives to your location, and details your car. They upload before and after photos so you can see exactly what was done. You pay online — no cash, no hassle.

**What areas do you serve?**
> We currently serve Etobicoke and Mississauga (West GTA). We're expanding to Brampton, North York, and Scarborough in 2026.

**Can you service my car in underground condo parking?**
> Yes — it's one of our specialties. We use waterless and rinseless methods that don't require hose access or outdoor space. We'll bring insurance certificates if your building requires them.

**How is pricing determined?**
> Pricing is based on your vehicle type (sedan, SUV, pickup, etc.) and the service you choose. Larger vehicles cost more because they require more time and materials. All prices are shown upfront before you book.

**What are the subscription plans?**
> We offer 4 monthly plans from $59 to $299/month. Each includes a set number of services per month. Services don't roll over. You can skip, pause, or cancel anytime with 30 days notice.

**Are your providers insured?**
> Yes. GLEAM carries commercial general liability insurance ($2M per occurrence). All providers are independently insured and must carry a minimum $1M liability policy.

**What if I'm not happy with the service?**
> We'll send someone back to make it right at no charge. Every job is photo-documented, so we can review exactly what happened and ensure our quality standard is met.

**How do before/after photos work?**
> Your provider takes 5 photos before starting (front, rear, both sides, interior) and 5 matching photos after. These are uploaded to your account in real time and sent to you via SMS when the job is complete.

**Can I book for multiple vehicles?**
> Yes. You can add as many vehicles to your account as you like. Subscribers get 20% off a second vehicle's plan.

**How do I become a GLEAM provider?**
> Apply on our website. We review your experience, tools, insurance, and availability. Approved partners complete a training session and 3 supervised jobs before going independent.
